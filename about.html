About Amanda-Leigh

Amanda-Leigh is a coach, trainer and facilitator with over 25 years' experience in drama, theatre, and education. Her work integrates creativity, somatic intelligence, and psychological depth, grounded in her background as a dancer, choreographer, drama teacher, and trauma-informed psychotherapist.

Trained in generative coaching with Stephen Gilligan and Robert Dilts, Amanda brings a finely tuned sensitivity to the nervous system, relational dynamics, and the subtle pathways of healing. She supports individuals and groups to reconnect with their creative essence, navigate complexity, and return to presence, flow, and deep connection.

Through story, embodied practice, and generative dialogue, Amanda creates spaces that are both spacious and precise — guiding others to unlock insight, soften resistance, and create from a place of authenticity and deep connection. She believes transformation begins in relationship, imagination, and the courage to truly listen.
