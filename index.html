Who I Work With

I work with people who feel stuck — emotionally, creatively, or practically.

This includes:

  • Creatives who feel blocked, burned out, or disconnected from their flow
  • Parents feeling overwhelmed, stretched, or lost inside the demands of care
  • Sensitive, thoughtful people navigating transition, grief, anxiety, or exhaustion
  • Anyone longing to live with more ease, meaning, and inner alignment

You don't need to be "broken" to come.

You just need to feel that something wants to shift.

How I Work

My work is relational, intuitive, and deeply practical.

Together we:

  • Gently untangle what's keeping you stuck
  • Build inner safety, clarity, and trust in yourself
  • Restore access to creativity, agency, and emotional flow
  • Find ways forward that fit who you are, not who you think you should be

This is not about fixing you.

It's about helping you come home to your own movement and wisdom.

Sessions & Fees

I offer:

  • A free initial conversation — a chance to meet on Zoom and explore what's alive for you, and see if we're a good fit
  • Ongoing 1:1 sessions, offered on a sliding scale of £60–£80

Sessions are held online but one-to-one sessions are also offered in person if required.
